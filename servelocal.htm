#SERVELOCALAR

Why We Serve Local

Serving others is an opportunity to demonstrate the love of Christ to the world in a tangible way. Whether its delivering a meal, building a fence, mentoring a student or driving a pregnancy center mobile unit, service meets a physical need that builds a bridge for spiritual conversations and transformation. Whether done in public or private, locally or globally, we serve that God may be glorified.
